5 physics ML datasets

Top Quark Tagging Reference Dataset

HDF5

1.2M training jets (600k top, 600k QCD) and 400k test jets. Each jet contains up to 200 constituents represented as (pT, η, φ, E) 4-momenta. Generated with PYTHIA8 + DELPHES fast detector simulation.

Jet Tagging8 GBDownload available

Quark-Gluon Dataset (CMS Open Data)

HDF5

Jet images and particle lists derived from CMS Open Data collision events. 700k training jets, balanced quark/gluon classes.

Jet Tagging14 GBDownload available

PDEBench Navier-Stokes

HDF5

2D incompressible Navier-Stokes trajectories at multiple viscosities (ν=1e-3, 1e-4, 1e-5). Each trajectory is 1000 time steps on a 64×64 spatial grid.

Navier-Stokes42 GBDownload available

WeatherBench 2 (ERA5)

NetCDF

ERA5 reanalysis data at 1.5° resolution for global weather forecasting benchmarking. Covers 1979–2022. Standard train/validation/test splits defined by WeatherBench 2.

Weather & Climate3.6 TBDownload available

MD17

NPY

Ab-initio molecular dynamics trajectories for 8 small organic molecules (aspirin, benzene, ethanol, malonaldehyde, naphthalene, salicylic acid, toluene, uracil). Energies and forces computed at DFT level.

Molecular Dynamics500 MBDownload available
