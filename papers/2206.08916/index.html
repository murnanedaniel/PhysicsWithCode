Learning Local Equivariant Representations for Large-Scale Atomistic Dynamics

NeurIPS 2022Jun 17, 2022arXiv:2206.08916
Ilyes Batatia (University of Cambridge), David P. Kovacs (University of Cambridge), Gregor Simm (University of Cambridge)

Abstract

We present MACE, a higher-order message passing neural network for fast and accurate force fields. MACE achieves state-of-the-art accuracy on the MD17 benchmark and shows unprecedented performance on the 3BPA benchmark, demonstrating exceptional data efficiency and transferability.
