Energy Flow Networks: Deep Sets for Particle Jets

JHEP 2019Oct 11, 2018arXiv:1810.05165
Patrick T. Komiske (MIT), Eric M. Metodiev (MIT), Jesse Thaler (MIT)

Abstract

We introduce Energy Flow Networks (EFNs) and Particle Flow Networks (PFNs), two infrastructures for building jet classifiers based on the energy flow of the event. EFNs and PFNs are both infrared and collinear safe by construction. We demonstrate their performance on quark-gluon discrimination and top quark tagging.
